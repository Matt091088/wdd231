Gentizi Site Plan

Site Name

Gentizi Home & Deco — This name reflects a store focused on modern melamine furniture and home decoration. It is short, easy to remember, and shows quality.

Site Purpose

The site will present modern melamine furniture such as kitchens, TV panels, wardrobes, and custom-made furniture. Visitors will be able to browse products, get inspiration for decoration, and contact the store for quotes.

Scenarios

  • What types of wardrobes or kitchens can I buy at Gentizi?
  • Where can I find contact information to order custom furniture?

Color Scheme

  • Yellow (#FFCC00) — used for titles, buttons, and accents.
  • Dark Gray (#2E2E2E) — used for main text, headers.
  • Medium Gray (#C0C0C0) — borders and product boxes.
  • Light Gray (#F5F5F5) — page background and sections.

Typography

  • Arial — for body text (simple and readable).
  • Verdana — for headings and subheadings (clean and modern).
